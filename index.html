Project Overview

Welcome to the Humanoid Robot Project! Our goal is to design a highly advanced robot capable of human-like movement and interaction. This robot can be used in various industries such as healthcare, disaster response, and human-robot collaboration.

Features

  • Height: 1.8 meters
  • Weight: 89 kg
  • Sensors: LIDAR, Camera, IMU, Force Sensors
  • Actuators: Electric Motors, Hydraulic Actuators

Applications

The humanoid robot is designed for a range of applications, including:

  • Search and Rescue Missions
  • Disaster Response
  • Construction Assistance
  • Human-Robot Interaction
